# Version 0.1 Scope Freeze

**Status:** Approved and frozen
**Approved:** 2026-07-04
**Release concept:** The First Holding

Version 0.1 proves that the Emerald Isle workflow can repeatedly deliver
high-quality features. Its objective is no longer feature discovery.

## Approved Gameplay Scope

- Oats
- One dry-stone wall variant
- XML-only hand quern, only if research supports meaningful gameplay
- Milled oats
- One or two oat-based foods
- Release integration

No additional gameplay feature may enter Version 0.1 without an accepted ADR that
explains why the release cannot meet its goals without the scope increase, what is
displaced, and how risk remains bounded.

Research may narrow or reject a conditional feature. Removing unjustified scope
does not require an ADR. Research may not replace rejected scope with a new feature
without the scope-change ADR.

## Phase Gate

Research proceeds in this strict order:

1. Oats
2. Dry-stone walls
3. Hand quern
4. Milled oats
5. Oat-based foods

Each research document requires human review before the next begins. Feature
specifications and implementation remain prohibited until all five research gates
are complete and reviewed.
