## Art Brief

The Version 0.3 preview preserves the accepted wide isometric holding, dry-stone
construction, thatched cottage, flax, oats, linen work, central hearth, grounded
material palette, and quiet left-side title field. The linen worker now wears a
compact charcoal brat over the pale tunic. The cloak is pinned at the chest and
hangs vertically as practical weather protection, with no train, swept tail,
royal styling, fantasy ornament, or copied RimWorld runtime art.

The exact release typography reads `EMERALD ISLE`, `VERSION 0.3`, and
`THE BRAT CLOAK`.

## Provenance and License

OpenAI's built-in image-generation tool made a precise clothing edit to the
project-owned Version 0.2 preview source under Emerald Isle art direction. The
edit target was limited to the worker's garment; the holding composition and
existing project-owned scene remained the design reference. Codex then fit the
illustration to 1280x720, added exact text with locally installed fonts, applied
the established title treatment and border, and palette-optimized the PNG below
Steam Workshop's one-megabyte preview limit.

The source edit and render helper are ignored production intermediates rather
than runtime dependencies or committed source art. No third-party images or
copied game textures were used. The preview is governed by
[`CREATIVE_ASSETS_LICENSE.md`](../../../CREATIVE_ASSETS_LICENSE.md).
