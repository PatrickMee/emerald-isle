## Summary

The Brat Cloak adds one practical outer garment to the household clothing path.
It pairs visually with the linen tunic and gives early and mid-game colonies a
cheap cold-weather option without replacing parkas, dusters, or armor.

## Included Content

- All Version 0.2 content.
- A shell-layer brat cloak craftable at the Crafting Spot and vanilla tailoring
  benches from Fabric textiles.
- A low material and work cost, strong cold insulation relative to the duster,
  weak armor, and no heat-weather advantage.
- Original ground and worn art for Male, Female, Thin, Hulk, and Fat body types
  in all required directions.

The release contains no C#, Harmony patches, required DLC, new textile resource,
wool-definition patch, dye or rank system, research project, ideology behavior,
Irish-language player text, or specialist archaeological reconstruction claim.

## Installation

Subscribe on the Steam Workshop for automatic installation, or install manually:

1. Copy the staged `EmeraldIsle` folder into RimWorld's local `Mods` directory.
2. Confirm the resulting path contains `EmeraldIsle/About/About.xml` rather than
   an additional nested `EmeraldIsle` folder.
3. Enable **Emerald Isle** after **Core** and restart when prompted.

The separate Emerald Isle Developer Tools package is local-only and must never be
included in a public release.

## Upgrade and Existing Saves

Adding Version 0.3.0 to a Version 0.2 save makes the brat-cloak recipe and apparel
definition available without custom migration or serialized state. Back up
important saves before changing any mod list.

Removing Emerald Isle from a save containing its plants, items, apparel,
buildings, blueprints, bills, policies, stockpile filters, outfits, inventories,
or caravan contents can produce ordinary missing-definition warnings and content
loss. Remove or consume mod content first where practical.

## Compatibility and Intended Behavior

- RimWorld 1.6 Core is required; supported DLC add no Version 0.3-specific
  behavior.
- The brat accepts vanilla `Fabric` stuffs. Installed RimWorld 1.6 Core
  definitions place wool in that category.
- Wool provides more cold insulation than cloth. The brat is cheaper and quicker
  to make than both the parka and duster, warmer than the duster, less protective
  than either, and less warm than the parka.
- The brat and linen tunic occupy different apparel layers and can be worn
  together.
