# Playtesting Guide

Playtesting answers a hypothesis; it is not unstructured time in the game.

## Plan

Define build, feature state, participant profile, save/scenario, DLC/mod matrix,
session length, hypotheses, tasks, prohibited coaching, metrics, and stop conditions.
Use `templates/playtest-report.md` for each session.

## Observe

Record what players do and say separately from interpretation. Capture decision
points, comprehension, timing, resource/labor cost, failure/recovery, exploits,
friction, emotional/story outcomes, and log/performance anomalies. Do not change
the design mid-session without starting a new experiment condition.

## Analyze

Compare evidence to hypotheses and vanilla baseline. Segment defects, balance,
usability, cultural interpretation, and preference. One player's preference is not
a universal result; repeated confusion is evidence even when the feature functions.

## Decide

Pass, pass with conditions, revise and retest, or reject. Link actions to issues,
update the feature/risks/debt, and preserve the report. Release candidates include
fresh-player and experienced-player coverage appropriate to scope.
