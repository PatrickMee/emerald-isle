## Change Control

These files are stable, not immutable. A material change normally requires an ADR
that explains motivation, alternatives, compatibility and workflow impact, and
affected documents. Constitution and Design Bible changes also follow their native
amendment/versioning rules. Typographical corrections, broken links, and factual
maintenance that do not alter meaning may use ordinary review with an explicit
`non-semantic` label.

The `v0.0.0` tag is the immutable historical baseline. Later changes do not rewrite
that tag or imply that earlier releases followed newer rules.
