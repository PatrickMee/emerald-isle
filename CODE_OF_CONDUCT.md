# Code of Conduct

Emerald Isle welcomes respectful contributions from people of varied backgrounds
and experience.

## Expected Conduct

- Discuss ideas and evidence directly without attacking people.
- Give specific, actionable feedback and accept correction professionally.
- Respect privacy, consent, attribution, and cultural context.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing,
  deliberate disruption, and derogatory stereotypes.
- Treat disagreement about history or culture as a reason for better sourcing and
  qualified review, not hostility or claims of personal ownership over identity.

## Scope

This applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews, discussions,
playtests, release channels, and public spaces where someone represents the project.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the Founding Maintainer through an available
private GitHub contact or repository security-advisory channel. Do not disclose
sensitive reports publicly. Maintainers may clarify expectations, edit or remove
content, issue warnings, limit participation, or ban participants based on impact,
pattern, and safety. Conflicts involving the Founding Maintainer should be handled
by an independent platform moderator or subsequently appointed maintainer.

Enforcement decisions should protect affected people, preserve relevant evidence,
limit disclosure, and document project-level action without exposing private detail.
